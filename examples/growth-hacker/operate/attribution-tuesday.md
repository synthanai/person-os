## ⚙️ R.H.Y.T.H.M. Engineering

**[R]itual (The Mechanism)**
"Attribution Tuesday" - A strict 90-minute block every Tuesday morning dedicated exclusively to interrogating the Salesforce-to-Meta API data loop. No new campaigns can be launched during this window.

**[H]abitat (The Environment)**
Dual monitors. Left screen: Salesforce CRM raw deal flow. Right screen: Meta Ads Manager conversion API logs. Noise-canceling headphones.

**[Y]ield (The Capability Produced)**
It prevents the silent death of ad campaigns. If the offline conversion API breaks, the algorithm stops receiving success signals and slowly starts buying garbage traffic. This ritual guarantees the structural integrity of the feedback loop, saving tens of thousands of dollars a week in misattributed spend.

**[T]rigger (The Activation Condition)**
The weekly pipeline generation report hitting my inbox at 8:00 AM on Tuesday.

**[H]ardline (The Strict Boundary)**
I will not accept "Google Analytics says..." as an argument during the rest of the week. Unless the conversion is physically stamped in Salesforce and verified during Attribution Tuesday, I treat the data as a hallucination.

**[M]etabolic Cost (The Energy Trade)**
It is brutally boring spreadsheet work that true creatives despise. I trade the excitement of launching new ads for the mathematical certainty that the ads I am currently running aren't burning cash.
