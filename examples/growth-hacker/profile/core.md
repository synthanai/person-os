## 🧭 C.O.R.E. Synthesis

**[C]alling (The Problem You Solve)**
I mathematically engineer customer acquisition. I transition marketing from "creative brand awareness" into a deterministic, algorithmic pipeline that converts attention into measurable revenue.

**[O]rigin (The Defining Moment)**
I was a junior copywriter at an agency that won a massive industry award for a beautiful video campaign. The agency threw a party. The client went bankrupt 3 months later because the video generated zero sales. I realized that if creativity doesn't lower the Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC), it is corporate vanity, not marketing.

**[R]eason (The Philosophical Anchor)**
Data is the only valid judge of creativity. A terrible, ugly landing page that converts at 5% is infinitely more valuable to the enterprise than a beautiful, award-winning page that converts at 1%.

**[E]ndurance (Your Unique Leverage)**
The ability to divorce my ego from my ideas. I can launch 10 wildly different ad creatives, watch the data brutally murder the 9 I personally liked best, and aggressively scale the 1 ugly ad that the algorithm chose, without feeling any emotional loss.
