## 🏔️ G.R.O.W.T.H. Mapping

**[G]oal (The Objective Vector)**
Ascend to the "Head of Growth" title within 12 months, shifting from managing ad campaigns to managing the entire holistic funnel, including product onboarding.

**[R]eality (The Current State)**
Regarded as an elite "Media Buyer." I control the top of the funnel (Meta/Google Ads) perfectly, but my influence stops the moment the user creates an account. I am dependent on the Product team to retain the users I buy.

**[O]bstacle (The Resistance)**
The Product team views me exclusively as an external trafficker. They fiercely resist my attempts to A/B test the onboarding flow, viewing it as marketing encroaching on product terrain.

**[W]aypoint (The First Milestone)**
Successfully execute a cross-functional "Lifecycle Sprint." Prove that modifying the first 3 onboarding tooltips impacts Day-7 retention more than modifying the initial advertising creative.

**[T]actics (The Execution Steps)**
1. Learn SQL deeply enough to pull my own retention cohorts from the database without asking the data team.
2. Form an alliance with the Product Manager owning the free-trial experience by offering to fund their next experimental feature using my leftover ad budget.
3. Present a closed-loop ROI report to the CEO demonstrating how a 2% increase in onboarding conversion mathematically saves $150k in ad spend over the quarter.

**[H]orizon (The Long Term View)**
A Media Buyer buys clicks. A Head of Growth buys the entire customer lifecycle. To grow, I must control the physics of the product, not just the physics of the ads.
