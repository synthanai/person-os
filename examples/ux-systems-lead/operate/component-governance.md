## ⚙️ R.H.Y.T.H.M. Engineering

**[R]itual (The Mechanism)**
"The Governance Council" - A strict 45-minute asynchronous Slack ritual every Tuesday where I review all "Component Detachment" requests from the broader design and product teams.

**[H]abitat (The Environment)**
Asynchronous. A dedicated Notion board linked to a specific Slack channel. Verbal requests are ignored. You must submit a component detachment request ticket with mathematical justification.

**[Y]ield (The Capability Produced)**
It protects the structural integrity of the codebase. It forces designers and PMs to prove that their "unique problem" actually requires custom code, defending the engineering team's capacity from death-by-a-thousand-custom-buttons.

**[T]rigger (The Activation Condition)**
Checking the specified Notion board every Tuesday at 1:00 PM.

**[H]ardline (The Strict Boundary)**
I will automatically reject any request for custom UI components if the requester cannot explicitly prove why the existing atomic components fail to solve the user's friction. "It looks better" is an instant rejection.

**[M]etabolic Cost (The Energy Trade)**
It makes me the most disliked designer in the room for the junior staff. I trade my popularity with the creatives for absolute trust from the engineering department.
