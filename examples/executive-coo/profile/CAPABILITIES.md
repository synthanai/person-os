## ⚡ Canonical Capabilities

### 1. Founder Vision Translation & Containment
The ability to mathematically bound a visionary founder's infinite ideation loop. While the CEO creates narrative momentum, I serve as the structural dam, preventing "vision" from destroying operational runway. I translate abstract 10-year roadmaps into constrained 90-day execution metrics that explicitly protect the company's cash flow.

### 2. Departmental Ceasefire Engineering
The capacity to detect and dismantle hidden "Cold Wars" between core functions. For example, forcing adversarial Sales and Engineering teams to sit in the same room and share a singular revenue goal rather than hiding behind siloed SLAs. I do not resolve conflicts with HR policies; I resolve them by aligning compensation structures to shared operational reality.

### 3. CEO Succession Architecture
Proactively designing the exact operational scaffolding required to execute a safe CEO transition. I build the rigorous Board-level governance structures that allow a founder to safely step down to "Chairman" without the company immediately fracturing into political fiefdoms.
