## 🛠️ Combat Skills

### 1. The Friday "Kill Board" Execution
*Mechanics:* Establishing a ruthless, unmovable 1-hour recurring ritual to manually cancel "zombie initiatives." Reviewing the roadmap and explicitly executing any project that has slipped its deadline twice or missed its revenue target by 20%. Forcing department heads to justify existence rather than rewarding them for effort.

### 2. Metric Unification
*Mechanics:* Taking opposing, siloed metrics (e.g., Sales "Booked Revenue" vs. Engineering "Uptime") and destroying them in favor of a singular, shared existential metric. (e.g., Forcing Sales to only take commission when a customer successfully deploys the software).

### 3. Asymmetric Information Gathering
*Mechanics:* Bypassing the traditional chain of command. Refusing to read sterilized "Executive Summaries" and instead sitting at the desk of a Junior SDR or Tier-1 Support Agent to hear the raw, unedited friction of the actual product.
