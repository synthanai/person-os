## ⚙️ R.H.Y.T.H.M. Engineering

**[R]itual (The Mechanism)**
"The Kill Board" - A ruthless 60-minute session every Friday at 4:00 PM designed exclusively to assassinate scope creep and terminate unviable projects before the weekend.

**[H]abitat (The Environment)**
The main glass conference room, standing only. A physical whiteboard marked into three columns: "Funded," "Failing," "Killed."

**[Y]ield (The Capability Produced)**
Ruthless prioritization. It protects the engineering organization's focus by forcing leadership to explicitly murder bad ideas rather than letting them slowly drain the company's capital.

**[T]rigger (The Activation Condition)**
The weekly burn-rate report hitting my inbox at 3:30 PM. 

**[H]ardline (The Strict Boundary)**
No emotional arguments are permitted in the room. Sentences starting with "But I feel like..." are immediately cut off. Decisions are made entirely on data, burn multiple, and alignment with the locked 90-day sprint.

**[M]etabolic Cost (The Energy Trade)**
Extreme friction with the visionary founder and sales leadership who want every project to live. I trade my personal popularity for the company's financial solvency.
