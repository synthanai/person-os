# Executive (Scale-up COO): Experience Ledger

**Timestamp**: 2026-04-17
**Framework**: EXPERIENCE (S.T.O.R.Y.)
**Domain**: Executive (Scale-Up COO)
**Milestone**: Breaking the Engineering/Sales Cold War

---

## 📈 S.T.O.R.Y. Extraction

**[S]ituation (The Baseline Reality)**
A Series B funded company was bleeding cash. The sales team was closing deals by selling "roadmap features" that engineering hadn't even scoped yet. 

**[T]ask (The Directive)**
Bridge the massive divide between Sales and Engineering, establish product-led growth metrics, and stop the churn of enterprise clients who were angry about undelivered features.

**[O]bstacle (The Complexity)**
A foundational breakdown of trust. Engineering viewed Sales as liars who promised impossible timelines; Sales viewed Engineering as slow, incompetent bottleneck to revenue.

**[R]esult (The Output)**
I took the most drastic action possible: I fired the top-performing salesperson who brought in 30% of the revenue but routinely lied to clients about features. I instituted a rigid "Sell What Exists" policy and placed engineering leads on pre-sales calls to enforce technical reality.

**[Y]ield (The Capability Gained)**
I learned the ultimate organizational truth: Culture is not what you say; it is what you tolerate. Firing the top performer physically changed the culture overnight. Engineering realized management had their back, and Sales realized honesty was the baseline. True alignment requires shedding toxic mass.
