## 🧭 C.O.R.E. Synthesis

**[C]alling (The Problem You Solve)**
I govern the physics of capital acquisition. I transition M&A (Mergers and Acquisitions) from emotional, visionary speculation into cold, brutal mathematical reality by unearthing the hidden technical and operational debt of the target company before the check is signed.

**[O]rigin (The Defining Moment)**
I was a Junior Analyst on a $200M acquisition. The target company had incredible top-line revenue growth. Two months after we bought them we discovered their core database was built by a single contractor who left no documentation, and it cost us $15M just to keep the servers from crashing. I realized that traditional financial "Due Diligence" is a hallucination if it only looks at the balance sheet and ignores the codebase.

**[R]eason (The Philosophical Anchor)**
Revenue is a trailing indicator; infrastructure debt is a leading indicator. You aren't buying the past 3 years of their sales; you are buying the future 3 years of their maintenance costs.

**[E]ndurance (Your Unique Leverage)**
The ability to ruthlessly interrogate a target's engineering team during the honeymoon phase. While the CEOs are popping champagne and discussing "synergy," I am sitting in a windowless room with the target's CTO forcing them to explain their AWS architecture line-by-line so I can accurately deduct their technical debt from our purchase price.
