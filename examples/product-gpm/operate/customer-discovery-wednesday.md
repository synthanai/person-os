## ⚙️ R.H.Y.T.H.M. Engineering

**[R]itual (The Mechanism)**
"Discovery Wednesday" - I do not look at Jira, Figma, or internal roadmaps on Wednesdays. The entire day is physically blocked off for 30-minute Zoom interviews with active customers, churned customers, and prospects who chose a competitor.

**[H]abitat (The Environment)**
Zoom. A dedicated Notion database specifically for raw transcription dumps. The engineering leads are invited as silent observers.

**[Y]ield (The Capability Produced)**
Unassailable market truth. When the CEO demands a feature because "he has a gut feeling," I can counter with 4 hours of raw video evidence of paying customers explicitly asking for the opposite. It converts Product Management from an opinion-based hierarchy into a data-driven science.

**[T]rigger (The Activation Condition)**
A Calendly link sent out via automated email campaigns every Monday morning to a target segment of the user base.

**[H]ardline (The Strict Boundary)**
I will never cancel a customer interview for an internal sync meeting. Internal meetings create consensus; customer interviews create revenue.

**[M]etabolic Cost (The Energy Trade)**
It is deeply exhausting to listen to people complain about the software you spent 6 months building. It requires suppressing the ego and the urge to "defend" the product. I trade my comfort for raw signal.
