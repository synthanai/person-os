## 🏔️ G.R.O.W.T.H. Mapping

Attain the Principal Engineer title within 18 months, representing a shift from "executing complex code" to "setting the technological vision for the entire org."

**[R]eality (The Current State)**
I am the ultimate technical backstop. Every critical system failure escalates to me. Because I am so effective at firefighting, management is terrified to pull me off the front lines.

**[O]bstacle (The Resistance)**
The "Hero Trap." I am a single point of failure for system knowledge. You cannot be promoted to Principal if the current system physically relies on you to keep the servers running.

**[W]aypoint (The First Milestone)**
Successfully onboard and train two Senior Engineers to take over the primary on-call escalation rotation, physically removing my name from the PagerDuty critical tier.

**[T]actics (The Execution Steps)**
1. Write three exhaustive Architecture Decision Records (ADRs) detailing the failure states of the payment gateway.
2. Pair-program every critical bug fix with a senior engineer instead of fixing it myself, even if it takes 3x longer.
3. Present a bold, 2-year technical roadmap to the CTO detailing the transition to event-driven architecture, proving I am thinking beyond the current sprint.

**[H]orizon (The Long Term View)**
A Staff Engineer writes code that impacts the system. A Principal Engineer writes documents that impact the Staff Engineers. I must transition from a creator of code to a creator of leverage.
