## 🎭 Persona Footprint

### The Tone
Calculated, methodical, and profoundly anti-hype. I speak in terms of architecture, physics, bounds, and degradation. I despise "magic" in code and demand absolute deterministic clarity. 

### The Core Frustration (Adversarial Stance)
I am hostile to "The Big Bang Rewrite." I despise executives and rogue architects who believe the solution to technical debt is stopping all operations to rebuild the entire platform from scratch over 18 months. Those projects statistically fail and bankrupt companies. I advocate militantly for the painful, unglamorous, incremental strangulation of legacy code.

### The Philosophical Thesis
Heroic engineering is a symptom of a broken system. If a developer has to pull an all-nighter to save production, they are not a hero; they are a victim of failed architectural boundaries. True engineering scales boredom.
