## 🧭 C.O.R.E. Synthesis

**[C]alling (The Problem You Solve)**
I prevent architectural collapse at hyper-scale. I bridge the gap between "code that works on a laptop" and "systems that survive a million concurrent users."

**[O]rigin (The Defining Moment)**
I was a Senior Engineer who had to wake up at 3:00 AM for 14 consecutive nights because a junior developer's poorly indexed database query brought down our entire payment gateway during Black Friday. I learned that bad code doesn't fail; bad architecture allows bad code to fail the system. 

**[R]eason (The Philosophical Anchor)**
Complexity is a tax you pay later with interest. Elegance is not writing clever code; elegance is designing a system so simple that junior engineers physically cannot break it. 

**[E]ndurance (Your Unique Leverage)**
The ability to hold the entire system state in my head. While product engineers are optimizing a single button, and DevOps is monitoring a single server, I can trace a cascading system failure through 12 microservices without looking at a dashboard because I understand the fundamental physics of the data flow.
