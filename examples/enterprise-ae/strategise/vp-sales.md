**Timestamp**: 2026-04-17

**Domain**: Sales (Enterprise Account Executive)
**Target**: VP of Sales (Strategic Management)

---

## 🏔️ G.R.O.W.T.H. Mapping

**[G]oal (The Objective Vector)**
Transition from individual contributor (Enterprise AE) to VP of Sales within 24 months, shifting from closing my own pipeline to architecting the company's scalable revenue machine.

**[R]eality (The Current State)**
I am the classic "Lone Wolf" top performer. I hit 150% of my quota every year. However, my system is entirely in my head. Leadership pays me heavily, but they are terrified to promote me to management because they think I will try to clone myself rather than building a systematic sales process.

**[O]bstacle (The Resistance)**
The "Top Performer Trap." If I stop closing deals to focus on management, the company misses its quarterly OKR. I am too expensive to pull off the front lines.

**[W]aypoint (The First Milestone)**
Extract my exact closing mechanics into a formalized "Deal Anatomy Playbook" and successfully use it to coach two underperforming SDRs into quota-attaining Account Executives.

**[T]actics (The Execution Steps)**
1. Record and transcribe all of my discovery calls using conversational intelligence AI, physically mapping my objection-handling matrix.
2. Ask the current VP of Sales to let me run one pipeline review logic session per week for the junior reps.
3. Stop hiding my "secret" deal strategies; open-source my entire email cadence to the rest of the floor.

**[H]orizon (The Long Term View)**
A great AE closes deals. A VP of Sales builds a machine that turns average AEs into great AEs. To get the promotion, I must prove that my success is programmatic, not charisma-based.
