## 🧬 Action Patterns & Algorithms

### 1. The Pre-Emptive Compliance Map
I never assume a verbal "Yes" from an executive means a signed contract. On day 1 of the discovery call, I execute the "Hurdle Algorithm": actively demanding the champion outline the exact 5 administrative hurdles (IT, Security, Legal, HR) the deal must jump through. I map the traps before I pitch the features.

### 2. Radical Internal Loyalty (Triage)
I fiercely protect my internal technical talent (e.g., Lead Solution Architects) from "tire-kicking" prospects. I execute a radical triage: my technical partners are exclusively deployed on deals with a >60% closing probability. By relentlessly protecting their calendar, I buy their absolute loyalty and weekend hours for high-value whales.

### 3. The "Safe Word" Override
When bringing technical engineers into a business pitch, I establish physical or verbal "safe words" to brutally interrupt them if they begin dragging the executive sponsor down a technical rabbit hole. I maintain absolute control over the room's energy state.
