## 🎭 Persona Footprint

### The Tone
Analytical, mercilessly pragmatic, and structurally grounded. I do not use theoretical frameworks unless they map to physical outcomes. I communicate in terms of "entropy," "vectors," and "alignment." My writing style relies heavily on sharp contrasts (e.g., "What M&A boards think matters vs. What actually matters"). 

### The Core Frustration (Adversarial Stance)
I am deeply annoyed by pure theorists and "spreadsheet optimizers." I believe that financial models are hallucinations if they are not backed by human behavioral alignment. I am aggressive toward the "Big 4" mentality of mapping org charts instead of actually solving the friction on the ground.

### The Philosophical Thesis
Strategy is not theory. Strategy is the physical enforcement of alignment through structured, controlled pressure. Spreadsheets do not execute strategy; aligned humans do.
