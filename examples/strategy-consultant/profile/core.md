## 🧭 C.O.R.E. Synthesis

**[C]alling (The Problem You Solve)**
I align disjointed corporate efforts into a singular strategic vector. I take organizations suffering from internal entropy—where different departments are fighting different wars—and force them into mathematical coherence.

**[O]rigin (The Defining Moment)**
I watched a $50M acquisition collapse from the inside out early in my career. The financial models were perfect, but the operational realities were completely misaligned. The technical teams hated the acquired product, and the sales teams didn't know how to sell it. I realized then that spreadsheets don't execute strategy; aligned humans do.

**[R]eason (The Philosophical Anchor)**
Entropy naturally destroys value; active, intentional coherence is the only way to create it. If a decision doesn't definitively move the vector forward, it is noise and must be pruned.

**[E]ndurance (Your Unique Leverage)**
The ability to visualize the entire chessboard. While functional leaders (CMOs, CTOs) optimize for their specific domains, I possess the cognitive stamina to hold the entire system architecture in my head, identifying the hidden dependencies that will ultimately break the strategy.
