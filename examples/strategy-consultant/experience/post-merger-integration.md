# Strategy Consultant: Experience Ledger

**Timestamp**: 2026-04-17
**Framework**: EXPERIENCE (S.T.O.R.Y.)
**Domain**: Strategy (Management Consultant)
**Milestone**: Hostile Post-Merger Integration

---

## 📈 S.T.O.R.Y. Extraction

**[S]ituation (The Baseline Reality)**
A legacy healthcare hardware manufacturer acquired a nimble software-as-a-service (SaaS) startup. The intention was to modernize their offerings. Reality: The hardware company's established, slow-moving sales process was suffocating the software company's agile delivery model. Turnover in the acquired startup hit 30% within three months.

**[T]ask (The Directive)**
Unify the go-to-market (GTM) strategy and stop the bleeding of top talent within a 90-day window, preventing a complete collapse of the investment thesis.

**[O]bstacle (The Complexity)**
Extreme cultural tribalism. The hardware executives viewed the software team as "undisciplined children," while the software team viewed the hardware executives as "dinosaurs." Data systems were completely siloed, making centralized reporting impossible and breeding deep mistrust regarding revenue numbers. 

**[R]esult (The Output)**
Executed a "Crucible Workshop" pulling the top 5 leaders from both divisions into a locked-room scenario. Forcibly mapped out a hybrid sales cycle that protected the software team's agility while satisfying the hardware team's compliance needs. Delivered a unified product roadmap and stanched the attrition rate to under 5% over the following quarter. Saved an estimated $15M in operational redundancy and lost sales.

**[Y]ield (The Capability Gained)**
Developed the "Crucible Framework"—a replicable methodology for forcing behavioral alignment across hostile corporate cultures using constrained, high-pressure design sprints. I no longer try to slowly change culture; I force alignment through structured environmental pressure.
