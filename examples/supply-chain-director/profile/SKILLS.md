## 🛠️ Combat Skills

### 1. The Redundancy Audit
*Mechanics:* Executing a highly strict, offline monthly war-room session. Taking the physical Bill of Materials (BOM) and simulating the total bankruptcy of the top 3 primary suppliers. Legally mandating that every component with a >14-day lead time possesses a contracted secondary supplier before a new product can launch.

### 2. Asymmetric Routing Logistics
*Mechanics:* The technical skill to completely bypass traditional ocean-freight bottlenecks during crises by instantly re-routing WIP (Work In Progress) inventory to near-shore assembly nodes, paying a mathematical premium for truck velocity over ship capacity to preserve market share.

### 3. Product Lock Enforcement
*Mechanics:* Utilizing ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) systems to build immutable "Design Lock" dates. If the Head of Product attempts to change a design specification after the lock date, the system physically forces them to approve the specific dollar amount of scrapped inventory before the change order processes.
