# Skill: SOCIAL + H.S.P. Framework

> *"You are not your job title. You are the vector of your capability."*

The **SOCIAL + H.S.P.** framework is the terminal content synthesis engine. Every piece of external content generated by the Person OS is produced through this dual layer. SOCIAL mines the internal ledger across 6 angles. H.S.P. packages the result into a three-beat structure optimized for professional content.

---

## Part 1: SOCIAL Protocol (Mining Layer)

Use all 6 angles to mine the OS ledger before drafting. Even if you only deploy 2-3 in the final output, mine all 6 first.

| Letter | Angle | Source Artifact |
|--------|-------|-----------------|
| **S** | Story | `experience/*.md` - The most concrete, evidence-based narrative |
| **O** | Operations | `operate/*.md` - The specific rhythms and mechanisms that make their work distinctive |
| **C** | Contrast | `profile/CORE.md` [Reason] - What the market gets wrong, what they do instead |
| **I** | Instruction | `experience/*.md` [Yield] - The specific, replicable tactic the reader can action |
| **A** | Adversarial | `profile/CORE.md` [Calling] - The enemy of their philosophy. What they are against |
| **L** | Legacy | `strategise/*.md` [Horizon] - The long-term bet. What they are building |

---

## Part 2: H.S.P. Framework (Packaging Layer)

Every NARRATE output is structured using Hook, Suspense, Punch:

| Beat | Function | Rule |
|------|----------|------|
| **Hook** | Create cognitive dissonance or specific curiosity | One sentence. Do NOT start with "I". Must be counterintuitive or emotionally resonant. |
| **Suspense** | Deepen the tension the Hook created | Adds specificity: a number, a named failure mode, a vivid contrast. Makes the reader lean forward. |
| **Punch** | The resolution | Traceable to `experience/` [Yield]. Ends with forward momentum (what they are building, not just what they have done). |

## Quality Gates

Before finalizing any NARRATE output:

1. **Traceability**: Every factual claim maps to a prior artifact. Flag any sentence that cannot be traced.
2. **Generic Purge**: Remove every instance of these phrases:

| Banned Phrase | Replace With |
|---|---|
| "results-driven" | Name the specific result |
| "passionate about" | Describe the actual behavior |
| "proven track record" | Cite the specific proof |
| "leverage" | Name what is being applied and to what |
| "robust" | Name the specific resilience mechanism |
| "seamless" | Name what friction was eliminated |
| "transformative" | Name the specific before/after delta |
| "unlock" | Name what was previously inaccessible |
| "in today's fast-paced world" | Delete entirely |
| "not many people talk about this" | Delete entirely |

3. **Aloud Test**: Read the full output aloud. Any sentence that sounds unnatural in speech gets rewritten.
4. **AI Smell Test**: Does this sound like a senior practitioner? Or like a language model impersonating one? If the latter, rewrite until the voice is concrete, experiential, and direct.

## DO Write Like This

- "I watched a $47M acquisition recover $14.8M in 18 months after two leaders who despised each other co-signed a protocol they had built under constraint."
- "The system has had zero customer-facing incidents in three years. The ADR is still the first document I give new engineers."
- "Heroic engineering is a symptom. Boring engineering is the goal."

## DO NOT Write Like This

- "I am passionate about helping organizations unlock their full potential through data-driven strategies."
- "With a proven track record of delivering results, I bring a robust approach to complex challenges."
- "In today's fast-paced world, AI is transforming how professionals leverage their experience."
