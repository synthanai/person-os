## What Is Employee Kit?

Employee Kit is a collection of **open-source AI tools** for experienced working professionals. Each tool (a **kit**) is standalone, free to use, and built to solve a real professional problem.

The first kit  -  **Profile Kit**  -  helps you build three personalised AIs that understand how you actually operate, lead, and what you're building. Each profile produces its own PIC that goes into its own ChatGPT Project.

> **KIT** = **K**nowledge · **I**ntuition · **T**ooling
>
> - **Knowledge**  -  tools that help you synthesise and apply professional intelligence faster
> - **Intuition**  -  tools that develop your judgment, self-awareness, and leadership
> - **Tooling**  -  practical AI tools that make your professional work more effective

## Quick Start

**Want to build your Professional PIC right now?** → Go to **[Profile Kit](kits/profile-kit/)**

It takes ~20 minutes per profile. You'll get three personalised AI instruction sets to paste into ChatGPT Projects. Here's what happens:

```
You → Employee GPT - Operate (~20 min) → Operate PIC → ChatGPT Project → Your Operations AI
You → Employee GPT - Lead   (~20 min) → Lead PIC   → ChatGPT Project → Your Leadership AI
You → Employee GPT - Matter (~20 min) → Matter PIC → ChatGPT Project → Your Strategy AI
```

Your AI will:
- ✅ Work at your level of experience. No basic framing.
- ✅ Match how you actually make decisions, not how you think you should
- ✅ Know what you're building, not just what your job description says
- ✅ Stretch you toward what matters  -  gently, at the edges, not in your face
- ✅ Evolve its understanding as you change

## Philosophy

**Recognition → Revelation → Revolution**

You use AI every day. But AI treats you the same as every other professional on the planet. That's the problem. Your profile is the fix. It turns a generic tool into a partner that actually knows how you operate.

> **Every professional needs a Professional PIC**  -  your **P**ersona, **I**nterface, and **C**ompass. One for each dimension of how you work.

### Three PICs. Three Dimensions. Three AI Partners.

| Profile | What It Maps | Your AI Partner |
|---------|-------------|-----------------|
| 🏢 **Operate PIC** | How you absorb knowledge, decide, and deliver | Your **Operations AI**  -  matched to how you actually work |
| 👁️ **Lead PIC** | How you read people, influence, and build capability | Your **Leadership AI**  -  calibrated to your leadership style |
| 🌍 **Matter PIC** | Who you are beneath the title, your career capital, what you're building | Your **Strategy AI**  -  holds your legacy direction |

Each PIC is a separate conversation and a separate ChatGPT Project because **you show up differently in each mode**  -  how you operate isn't how you lead, and how you lead isn't who you are at the deepest level.

Read more: [Philosophy](docs/philosophy.md) · [The 3×3 Matrix](docs/3x3-matrix.md)

## The 3×3 Matrix

|  | 📥 Intelligence | ⚙️ Execution | 📤 Delivery |
|--|----------------|-------------|------------|
| 🏢 **Operate** | How you absorb knowledge | How you decide and ship | How you communicate outcomes |
| 👁️ **Lead** | How you read people | How you influence | How you build culture |
